# Sales Prediction Dashboard

## Overview
This project is a Power BI Sales Prediction Dashboard developed to analyze sales performance, monitor revenue trends, and forecast future business growth using historical sales data.

The dashboard provides interactive visualizations and KPI tracking to help understand monthly revenue patterns and business performance.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI Desktop
- Excel
- Data Visualization
- Forecast Analysis

---

## Features
- Total Revenue Analysis
- Average Monthly Revenue Tracking
- Revenue KPI Monitoring
- Monthly Sales Trend Visualization
- Next Month Revenue Prediction
- Interactive Filters & Slicers
- Business Insights Panel

---

## Key Insights
- Sales showed a steady upward trend over recent months.
- Minor revenue decline observed during October and December.
- Forecast analysis predicts stable revenue growth for the upcoming month.
- KPI indicators help compare actual revenue against business goals.
